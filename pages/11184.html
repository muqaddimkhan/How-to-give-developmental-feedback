Tips for Giving Developmental Feedback in English

Welcome to "Tips for Giving Developmental Feedback in English".

Columbus is an international company, which boasts a diverse array of languages spoken by our employees. English serves as our primary language of communication, so effective communication in English is paramount. Multilingual individuals may agree that conveying thoughts in one's native language comes naturally, yet when tasked with providing developmental feedback in a different language, such as English, additional attention to vocabulary and phrasing may be necessary.


This course aims to assist in navigating the complexity of delivering developmental feedback in English, ensuring clarity and minimizing misunderstandings in our global setting.
